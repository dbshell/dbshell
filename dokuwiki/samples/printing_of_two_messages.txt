===== Printing of two messages =====

This example prints out text "First message", than text "Second message". Texts are printed on standard output or to execution log, when executed from DbMouse.

<code xml>
<?xml version="1.0" encoding="utf-8" ?>
<Batch 
  xmlns="http://schemas.dbshell.com/core"
>
  <Echo Message="First message" />
  <Echo Message="Seconds message" />
</Batch>
</code>
